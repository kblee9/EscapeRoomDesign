Maneuver the Maze


Through the portal you go, immediately you step out into a dark and cold hallway made entirely of stone. The group of tourists stay huddled against a wall, eyes wide as you and the other Magicians appear seemingly out of thin air. After short introductions you scope out more of your surroundings. Some of the walls are covered in paintings of gruesome scenes, others in strange writing none of which is decipherable. There is no doorway, just tunnels that lead further into the shadows. There is no doubt that this is the mythical labyrinth. On the floor lays the end of a glowing golden string. With very little options, you decide to follow it.








use the keys to direct yourself to the center of the maze
